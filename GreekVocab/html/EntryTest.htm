Vocabulary Entry Test

To start select "All Files" or a single file from the dropdown list at top left.

The greek words are entered in the list on the left. Click on a word to select it. Enter your translation in the box at the bottom. The letters entered will be shown in green as long as they match the stored translation. If they don't match they will be shown in green. When the translation is completed correctly the word is shown in black and entered in the list on the right.

To see the answer click on "Show Translation".
